    Class Minimap

    Defined in: lib/minimap.coffee

    Overview

    Public: The Minimap class is the underlying model of a MinimapElement. Most manipulations of the minimap is done through the model. Any Minimap instance is tied to a `TextEditor`. Their lifecycle follow the one of their target `TextEditor`, so they are destroyed whenever their `TextEditor` is destroyed.
